Deadpool


Deadpool (born who-knows-when, because he's too cool to care) is an iconic anti-hero, known for his sarcastic humor, fourth-wall-breaking antics, and relentless combat skills. Deadpool is a leading figure in the world of unconventional superheroes, with a massive fanbase who adore his chaotic energy.

Originally a skilled mercenary named Wade Wilson, he gained his infamous regenerative healing factor after a highly questionable experiment. His adventures span across mercenary missions, comedic showdowns, and unexpected team-ups with the X-Men (even if they don’t always invite him). Deadpool has faced off against powerful villains while delivering non-stop quips, pop culture references, and, of course, maximum carnage. His story includes unforgettable battles, outrageous stunts, and the occasional taco break.

With his signature red-and-black suit, dual katanas, and an arsenal of weapons, Deadpool continues to redefine what it means to be a "hero"—or at least, whatever suits him at the moment.
